Authentic Javanese Art Expo

  • Authentic Javanese Art Expo

    Authenthic Javanese Art Expo is the first Virtual Javanese Art Expo in Indonesia. We will hold 4 performances in one venue like musical drama, batik exhibition, traditional dance and traditional music.






Batik Exhibition

Batik is one of Indonesia's cultural heritage which is the work of the Indonesian nation which is a blend of art and technology by the ancestors of the Indonesian nation. Various styles of batik that contain full meaning and philosophy from various customs and cultures that developed in Indonesia

Traditional Dance

Traditional dance is a dance that has long developed in people's lives and always describes the traditions and cultural patterns of the community. The variety of traditional dance arts in Indonesia has its own characteristics and uniqueness in each region which has become a culture for certain ethnicities and identities that are able to unite the socienty.

Traditional Music

Gamelan is the cultural root of Javanese society. Philosophically, gamelan is identical to the Javanese way of life. In Javanese society, gamelan has an aesthetic function related to social, moral, and spiritual values. Gamelan is a set of instruments that are sounded simultaneously, a combination of several musical instruments such as the gambang, gendang, gong, etc.

Drama Musical

Modern musical drama is a performing arts drama combined with several other arts such as dance and music where this art can be developed according to the demands of the times. All of these arts are then packaged in such a way as to form an interesting choreography and elevate folklore so that it will add a new experience for the audience.
